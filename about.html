About me

Hi! I am Anna Yoshinari, a third year student studying character animation at CalArts.

I am passionate about exploring non-photorealistic CG. I made a full CG animated film in my second year and I am currently working on a new film using Blender and Maya. I also love working on visual development, and creating a unique, specific look that matches each projects.

In my free time, I like to make clothes and do embroidery. I also enjoy painting using traditional mediums such as watercolor and gouache.
